The Profile of American Academy for Training Courses (AATC):

An Overview

The American Academy for Training Courses (AATC) was established in 2007. It has expanded to include 3 projects:

  • American Academy for Training Courses (AATC)
  • International Testing Center (ITC)
  • School Management and Educational Consulting Center (SMECC)

The American Academy for Training Courses (AATC)’s vision Statement:

To provide applying training programs of exceptional quality to help customers to achieve unlimited success in their professional/personal lives. The company’s collective aim is to be recognized as the best training provider in Egypt. American Academy for Training Courses (AATC)’s Mission:

To provide clients with a wide range of training courses that best suit their needs, and equipping professionals, teachers, students and parents with the qualifications necessary to fulfill their roles.

American Academy for Training Courses (AATC)’s values:

  • Strive toward customers & employees satisfaction and retention
  • Team, Organization, & Community Influence
  • Responsibility & respect
  • Strive for quality
  • Customer first
  • Innovative

American Academy for Training Courses (AATC) Curricula:

The American Academy curricula are designed to offer hands-on training in a variety of fields using the latest teaching methodologies, tools and technologies.
